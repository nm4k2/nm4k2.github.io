This documentary is the second of a series of documentary films directed by Michael Apt, who began shooting in 1964. Michael Apt in 1964 for the British BBC television filmed the documentary “7Up” interview, fourteen seven year olds from different classes in Britain, some of them from the orphanage some upper class children. Since then, every seven years, Apt will re interview those children, listen to their dreams, talk about their lives. After seven years, these innocent children have just grown up.
